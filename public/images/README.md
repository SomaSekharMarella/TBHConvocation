# Images Folder

Place your photos here:

- `photo1.jpg` - Will be displayed in the introduction section
- `photo2.jpg` - Will be displayed in the recruitments section  
- `photo3.jpg` - Will be displayed in the "From seniors to something more" section

You can use `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, or `.webp` formats.

The images will automatically be displayed in the story sections when you add them to this folder.
